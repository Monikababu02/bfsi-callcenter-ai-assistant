EMI Calculation:
EMI is calculated using principal amount, interest rate, and tenure.

Interest Rates:
Interest rates vary depending on loan type, customer credit score, and policy updates.

Late Payment Policy:
Late payments may attract penalty charges as per company policy.

Foreclosure Policy:
Customers may close loans early subject to foreclosure charges.

SECTION: EMI Calculation
EMI is calculated using:
EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]
Where:
P = Principal
R = Monthly Interest Rate
N = Tenure in months

SECTION: Interest Policy
Interest rates depend on credit profile and policy updates.

SECTION: Late Payment
Late payments may attract penalties as per company terms.
